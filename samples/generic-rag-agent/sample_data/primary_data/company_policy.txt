Company General Policy

Working Hours:
- Standard working hours: 9 AM to 5 PM
- Flexible hours available with manager approval
- Core hours (must be present): 10 AM to 3 PM

Remote Work:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must maintain availability during core hours
- Prior approval required from direct manager

Equipment and Technology:
- Company provides laptop and necessary peripherals
- Annual budget for home office equipment: $500
- Software purchases must be approved by IT department

Communication:
- Primary communication channel: Company Slack
- Email for formal communications
- Video calls preferred for team meetings

Data Security:
- All company data must be stored on approved cloud storage
- Use VPN when accessing company resources remotely
- Two-factor authentication required for all accounts
- Report security incidents immediately to IT

Expenses:
- Submit expense reports within 30 days
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Pre-approval needed for expenses over $500
